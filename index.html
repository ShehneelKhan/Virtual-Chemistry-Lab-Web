VIRTUAL CHEMISTRY LAB

Practice your practicals!

Introduction

This Project provide platform for the users where users can see himself in virtual lab without physically perform lab. Users will perform lab virtually without physical apparatus.
By using the virtual labs users can see the apparatus and use without physically trying them.

A big advantage for users is that when practicing users can attempt multiple times as apparatus is available online and can be just dragged or respawned on resetting experiment.
Many fields such as engineering, medical, gaming, military etc are using this technology.

The Problems it Solves

In those remote areas where students of matric level are facing difficulty in performing as well as in understanding chemistry lab.

The reason is that students in government schools and even in private schools do not practice practice lab properly because of the limited available apparatus.

Moreover, some students are unable to purchase such apparatus. Therefore, this platform is the solution for all of the above problems!
